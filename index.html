Our Vision

We envision a world where people breathe clean air, drink clean water, eat safe food, and live in an equitable and healthy environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to involve the people of Western New York in making decisions that will preserve the natural resources of our world for future generations.

We believe in the attainability of clean and cost-efficient energy from sustainable sources for our communities that will provide workforce education and training for jobs with living wages.

To achieve this, we will: educate about the urgent need for a transition to clean energy and a pollution-free environment by providing sound, evidence-based information that will drive efforts to mitigate the current and anticipated effects of climate change, and implement action-oriented and multi-pronged approaches to achieve the goals set out in local, State, US and Global commitments.

We look to partner with like-minded organizations in our communities to achieve these goals.

Recent Successes

In the most recent election, New Yorkers resoundingly supported our right to “clean air, clean water and a healthful environment.” Almost two million people (69% of voters) took a stand for the environment by voting “Yes” on Proposal 2 on November 2nd! This adds the right to clean air and water and a healthy environment to the Constitution of New York State. Thanks to all the CRP Leaders who posted on social media to spread the word!
